Privacy Policy for SloGlo Arcade
Last Updated: May 29, 2026
1. Introduction Welcome to SloGlo Arcade ("the App"). 
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information related to your use of the App. 
This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

2. Data Collection and Usage
* No Personal Data Collection: 
SloGlo Arcade does not collect, store, or transmit any personal identification information (such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical location).
* Local Storage Only: 
All game progression, high scores, current arcade credit balances, daily free top-up allocations, and unlocked custom theme purchases are saved locally on your physical device. This data is stored using standard iOS local storage mechanics and is never uploaded to an external server.
* No Third-Party Analytics or Tracking: 
The App does not contain third-party tracking software, analytics packages, or advertising SDKs.

3. Children's Privacy
Because SloGlo Arcade features simulated arcade and casino-style mechanics (using entirely fictional, free-to-play credits), 
the App is rated 17+/18+ on the App Store. 
However, because we do not collect any personal data whatsoever, 
we do not knowingly or actively collect any data from individuals of any age group, including children.

4. In-App Purchases and Real Gambling 
The App contains zero In-App Purchases, paid microtransactions, or real-money gambling features. 
Fictional credits cannot be exchanged for real money, services, or physical items.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. 
Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
